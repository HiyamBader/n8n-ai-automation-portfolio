# 08 - AI Customer Support Email Assistant

## Goal

Build an AI-powered customer support assistant that reads incoming emails, detects the customer's intent, generates a professional reply draft, requests human approval, and sends the approved response.

## Features

- Monitor new Gmail messages
- Normalize email data
- AI intent detection
- Route emails by category
- Generate AI reply drafts
- Human approval before sending
- Send approved email replies
